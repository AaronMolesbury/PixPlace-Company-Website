About Us

PixPlace is an image sharing social hub, with an emphasis on photo challenges and earning points to rise the ranks in an online leaderboard.

PixPlace allows users to share photos they have taken of animals and objects (sharing photos of people is forbidden) with others. Photos can receive feedback and points from other users. Users can also tag the location of where they took the photo, allowing others to attempt to photograph the same animal/object, competing against other to see who can take the picture that gets the most points.
