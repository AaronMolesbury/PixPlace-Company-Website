Video not working? Try watching directly on YouTube: Image Labelling 


            Using Firebase machine learning, PixPlace will detect entities in your images and provide you 
            with a menu of labels to use as your image’s tag with over 10,000 available labels in many categories. 

            As seen in the above video, the image labelling is integrated with PixPlace using both the application’s 
            in-app camera (Android emulator) as well as the device’s camera. Due to the simplistic nature of the emulator's
            virtual room, the image labelling can pick up many different entities, however it has an easier time narrowing 
            down the more accurate detected labels with images taken in the real world.

            The labelling feature cannot currently be implemented with PixPlace's uploads from the gallery, however the 
            video shows an example of what that might look that if it could become a part of the app.
